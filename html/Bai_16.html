Bài 16. Hệ phương trình Euler, bài toán Riemann, phân rã gián đoạn Godunov¶

[Godunov - Phng php s gii cc bi ton Kh ng la hc nhiu chiu]

Yous Van Halder - Godunov method for the Euler Equations

1. Phương trình Euler¶

Dng chy lu cht khng nht c m t bi h phng trnh Euler. H phng trnh i vi dng chy mt chiu c dng: $$\frac{\partial U}{\partial t} + \frac{\partial F}{\partial x} = 0 \qquad(1)$$ trong : \begin{align} U & = \begin{pmatrix} \rho \\ \rho u \\ \rho e\end{pmatrix}, & F & = \begin{pmatrix} \rho u \\ \rho u^2 + p \\ \rho ue + pu\end{pmatrix}. \end{align}

vi $\rho$ - khi lng ring, $u$ - vn tc theo phng x, $p$ - p sut, $e$ - nng lng mt n v khi lng lu cht. Nng lng $e$ gm hai thnh phn: ni nng $\varepsilon$ v ng nng $u^2/2$: $$e = \varepsilon + \frac{u^2}{2}$$ Ta thy c ba phng trnh nhng c ti 4 n. 'khp kn' h ta s dng thm phng trnh trng thi: $$p=p(\rho, \varepsilon)$$ i vi kh l tng: $$p = (\gamma-1)\rho\varepsilon \qquad (2)$$ vi $\gamma$ l s m on nhit. Trng hp kh l khng kh (xem nh kh hai nguyn t) th $\gamma=1.4$.

Phng trnh Euler m t ba nh lut bo ton khi lng, ng lng v nng lng. Trong trng hp dng chy ba chiu ta c thm hai thnh phn vn tc $v$ v $w$, h c dng:

$$\frac{\partial U}{\partial t} + \frac{\partial F}{\partial x} + \frac{\partial G}{\partial x} +\frac{\partial H}{\partial x} = 0\qquad(3)$$

vi \begin{align} U & = \begin{pmatrix} \rho \\ \rho u \\ \rho v \\ \rho w \\ \rho e\end{pmatrix}, & F & = \begin{pmatrix} \rho u \\ \rho u^2 + p \\ \rho u v \\ \rho u w \\ \rho ue + pu\end{pmatrix} & G & = \begin{pmatrix} \rho v \\ \rho uv \\ \rho v^2 + p \\ \rho v w \\ \rho ve + pv\end{pmatrix} & H & = \begin{pmatrix} \rho w \\ \rho uw \\ \rho vw \\ \rho w^2 + p \\ \rho we + pw\end{pmatrix}. \end{align} v $e = \varepsilon + \frac{u^2+v^2+w^2}{2}$

Chng ta gii phng trnh Burger cng c dng ging phng trnh (1). gii h phng trnh Euler 1D cm gic nh ch cn thay u v hng trong phng trnh Burger bng vector U l n. Th nhng mi th khng n gin nh vy. Mu cht vn l gii quyt bi ton phn r gin on trn b mt th tch kim sot hay l bi ton Riemann nh th no. Godunov chnh l ngi u tin a ra li gii chnh xc cho bi ton ny (1959).

2. Phân rã gián đoạn Godunov¶

Xt bi ton Riemann trong ng lc hc lu cht: $$U_t + F(U)_x = 0 \qquad (4)\\ U(t=0)= \begin{cases} U_L(\rho_L, u_L, p_L); \quad x<x_0 \\ U_R(\rho_R, u_R, p_R); \quad x>x_0 \end{cases} $$

Biến bảo toàn¶

Phng trnh (1) hay (4) c vit di dng bo ton (dng phn k) cho bin bo ton U v dng F. Ma trn Jacobian $A=\frac{\partial F}{\partial U}$:

$$A= \begin{pmatrix} 0 & 1 &0\\ \frac{1}{2}(\gamma-3)u^2 & (3-\gamma)u & (\gamma-1) \\ \frac{1}{2}(\gamma-2)u^3-\frac{c^2u}{\gamma-1} & \frac{c^2}{\gamma-1}+(\frac{3}{2}-\gamma)u^2 & \gamma u \end{pmatrix} $$

vi vn tc m thanh $$c=\sqrt{\frac{\gamma p}{\rho}}$$

Matrn A c 3 gi tr ring: $$\lambda_1 = u-c, \lambda_2 = u, \lambda_3 = u+c$$ Vi 3 tr ring $\lambda_1<\lambda_2<\lambda_3$, h phng trnh c dng hyperbolic. Tng ng ta c 3 vector ring:

$$ \begin{align} R_1 & = \begin{pmatrix} 1\\ u-c \\ H-uc \end{pmatrix} & R_2 & = \begin{pmatrix} 1\\ u \\ u^2 \end{pmatrix} & R_3 & = \begin{pmatrix} 1\\ u+c \\ H+uc \end{pmatrix} \end{align} $$

Vi H l enthalpy: $$H = \frac{\rho(\varepsilon + u^2/2) + p}{\rho}=\frac{1}{2}u^2+\frac{c^2}{\gamma-1}$$

Biến nguyên thủy (biến gốc)¶

Phng trnh (1) c th vit li dng khng bo ton (dng khng phn k - xem bi 12) cho bin nguyn thy $P=(\rho, u, p)$. Khi ma trn Jacobian:

$$A= \begin{pmatrix} u & \rho & 0\\ 0 & u & \frac{1}{\rho} \\ 0 & \rho c^2 & u \end{pmatrix} $$

c 3 tr ring: $$\lambda_1 = u-c, \lambda_2 = u, \lambda_3 = u+c$$ 3 vector ring: $$ \begin{align} R_1 & = \begin{pmatrix} 1\\ -\frac{c}{\rho} \\ c^2 \end{pmatrix} & R_2 & = \begin{pmatrix} 1\\ 0 \\ 0 \end{pmatrix} & R_3 & = \begin{pmatrix} 1\\ \frac{c}{\rho} \\ c^2 \end{pmatrix} \end{align} $$

Ba tr ring tng ng vi 3 gin on, vi $\lambda_2 = u$ khng ph thuc $\rho, p$ - gin on ny gi l gin on tip xc, hai gin on cn li c th l sng xung kch hoc sng gin. Ta c cu hnh gin on nh sau:

Bn cu hnh u tin u cha gin on tip xc, ti y khi lng ring b 'gin on' cn vn tc v p sut th 'lin tc'. Gi tr ca chng bn tri v phi nh nhau ta k hiu l $P, U$. Cn khi lng ring v ni nng bn tri, phi ta k hiu $R_I, E_I$ v $R_{II}, E_{II}$. Hai vng tri phi ngoi cng l vng khng nhiu ng vi cc thng s $(p_I, \rho_I, u_I), (p_{II}, \rho_{II}, u_{II})$. Cu hnh cui cng l trng hp xut hin vng chn khng gia hai sng gin, ni khi lng gim xung bng 0.

Phng php gii:

Qu trnh bin i ton hc cc bn tm hiu thm trong quyn sch ca Godunov. Phng php gii c tm tt nh sau: Gi s gin on lan truyn vi vn tc $D=dx/dt$. p dng nh lut bo ton cho mt th tch hu hn bao quanh mt on ng gin on ta c: $$ [\rho]D - [\rho u] = 0 \qquad (I)\\ [\rho u]D - [p+\rho u^2] = 0 \qquad(II)\\ [\rho(\varepsilon+u^2/2)]D - [\rho u(\varepsilon+u^2/2)+pu] = 0 \qquad(III) $$ vi k hiu [] - chnh lnh gi tr hai bn gin on. i vi sng xung kch, bin i ta c: $$E-\varepsilon-\frac{1}{2}(1/\rho-1/R)(p+P) = 0$$ vi $(p,\rho, \varepsilon)$ - i lng pha trc sng xung kch, $(P, R, E)$ - pha sau sng xung kch. Loi b ni nng bng phng trnh trng thi ta thu c iu kin RankineHugoniot: $$R=\rho\frac{(\gamma+1)P + (\gamma-1)p}{(\gamma-1)P + (\gamma+1)p}$$

Xt sng xung kch bn tri, t phng trnh (I, II, III) ta c mt dng khi lng hay vn tc khi lng: $$a_I = \rho_I(u_I-D_I) = R_I(U-D_I)$$ Bin i ta c: $$U - u_I + \frac{P-p_I}{a_I} = 0$$ $$a_I = \sqrt{\rho_I(\frac{\gamma+1}{2}P +\frac{\gamma-1}{2}p_{I})} \qquad(8)$$

i vi sng xung kch bn phi:

$$a_{II} = \rho_{II}(D_{II}-u_{II}) = R_{II}(D_{II}-U)$$$$U - u_{II} - \frac{P-p_{II}}{a_{II}} = 0$$$$a_{II} = \sqrt{\rho_{II}(\frac{\gamma+1}{2}P +\frac{\gamma-1}{2}p_{II})} \qquad(9)$$

Trng hp sng gin tri, phi: $$a_{I} = \frac{\gamma-1}{2\gamma}\rho_Ic_I\frac{1-P/p_{I}}{1-(P/p_{I})^{(\gamma-1)/(2\gamma)}} \qquad(10)$$ $$a_{II} = \frac{\gamma-1}{2\gamma}\rho_{II}c_{II}\frac{1-P/p_{II}}{1-(P/p_{II})^{(\gamma-1)/(2\gamma)}}\qquad(11)$$ Vic u tin chng ta cn lm l xc nh p sut P. C hai cch tm nghim P chnh xc: phng php lp v gii phng trnh f(P) = 0. Ngoi ra cn c cc phng php xp x tm P.

Phng php lp:

Gi s $P^{n-1}$ l nghim gn ng ca P ($P^0$ ti thi im ban u cho trc). Khi $a_{I}^{n-1}$ tnh theo cng thc (8) nu $P^{n-1} \geq p_I$, theo cng th (10) nu ngc li, cn gi tr $a_{II}^{n-1}$ tnh theo cng thc (9) nu $P^{n-1} \geq p_{II}$ v theo cng thc (11) nu ngc li.

Sau gi tr $P^n$ xc nh theo cng thc: $$P^n = \varphi(P^{n-1})=\frac{a_{II}p_I + a_Ip_{II}+a_Ia_{II}(u_I-u_{II})}{a_I+a_{II}}$$

Tip tc qu trnh lp cho ti khi nghim hi t.

Kim chng cho thy nghim hi t khi sau phn r khng xut hin sng gin rt mnh. Do ta thay i qu trnh lp nh sau ph hp vi mi trng hp:

$$P^n=\frac{\alpha_{n-1}P^{n-1} + \varphi(P^{n-1})}{1+\alpha_{n-1}}$$$$\alpha_{n-1}= \begin{cases} \large \frac{\gamma-1}{3\gamma}\frac{1-z_{n-1}}{\large\large{z_{n-1}^{(\gamma+1)/(2\gamma)}(1-z_{n-1}^{(\gamma-1)/(2\gamma)})}}-1, if >0\\ o, otherwise \end{cases} $$$$z_{n-1}=\frac{P^{n-1}}{p_I+p_{II}}$$

Gii phng trnh f(P) = 0

Ngoi s dng phng php lp ta c th tm P bng cch gii phng trnh:

$$F(P) = f_I + f_{II} + u_{II} - u_I = 0$$$$f_k = \begin{cases} \large \frac{P-p_k}{\rho_kc_k\sqrt{\frac{\gamma+1}{2\gamma}\large \frac{P}{p_k} + \frac{\gamma-1}{2\gamma}}}; P\geq p_k\\ \large \frac{2}{\gamma-1}c_k(\frac{P}{p_k}^{\frac{\gamma-1}{2\gamma}} - 1); P < p_k \end{cases} $$

Cc cng thc xc nh cc i lng cn li: $$U=\frac{a_Iu_I+a_{II}u_{II}+p_I-p_{II}}{a_I+a_{II}}$$

Sng xung kch tri: $$D_I = u_I - \frac{a_I}{\rho_I}$$ $$R_I = \frac{\rho_I a_I}{a_I-\rho_I(u_I-U)}$$ Sng phn r tri c hai vn tc cn xc nh tng ng ti 'cnh trc v cnh sau' hnh r qut: $$D_I=u_I-c_I, D_I^*=U-c_I^*, c_I^*=c_I+(\gamma-1)/2(u_I-U)$$ $$R_I=\gamma\frac{P}{(c_I^*)^2}$$

Tng t cho gin on bn phi.

Sng xung kch phi: $$D_{II} = u_{II} + \frac{a_{II}}{\rho_{II}}$$ $$R_I = \frac{\rho_{II} a_{II}}{a_{II}+\rho_{II}(u_{II}-U)}$$ Sng phn r phi: $$D_{II}=u_{II}+c_{II}, D_{II}^*=U+c_{II}^*, c_{II}^*=c_{II}-(\gamma-1)/2(u_{II}-U)$$ $$R_{II}=\gamma\frac{P}{(c_{II}^*)^2}$$

Gi tr xp x ban u $P^0$ c th tnh nh sau:

$$P^0=\frac{p_I\rho_{II}c_{II}+p_{II}\rho_Ic_I+(u_I-u_{II})\rho_Ic_I\rho_{II}c_{II}}{\rho_Ic_I+\rho_{II}c_{II}}$$

Nghiệm chính xác¶

Nghim chnh xc ca bi ton c xc nh cn c vo v tr ca cc gin on ti thi im t. Khi , im $x_i$ thuc vng no th nhn gi tr $(\rho, u, p)_i$ ca vng .

Sơ đồ Godunov¶

i vi s Godunov ta gii bi ton Riemann cho tt cc k tip nhau tm gi tr ln trn b mt gia hai . Sau khi thu c cc gi tr LN cc vng, xc nh gi tr SAO ti mt $i+1/2$, cng ging nh trong bi 12, chng ta phi cn c vo v tr ca mt nm vng no trong cu hnh gin on.

Điều kiện ổn định sơ đồ Godunov¶

S n nh khi tha mn iu kin: $$CFL = u_{max}\frac{\Delta t}{\Delta x} < 1$$ voi $u_{max}$ m vn tc dao ng ln nht trong dng chy.

Bài toán¶

kim tra s ta xt 4 bi ton trn on [0,1], chia tri phi ti im $x^*$ trong khong thi gian $t_{max}$. Ring bi ton 4 xt trong iu kin $\gamma = 9$.

Code¶

S dng cc k t:

P - primitive variables: $P=(r, u, p)$; Ps - mng P

U - conserved variables: $U=(r, ru, re)$; Us - mng U

F - fluxes : $F=(fr, fu, fe)$

Điều kiện biên¶

Chng ta s s dng iu kin bin 'chy ra' - outflow: $Ps[0] = Ps[1], Ps[-1] = Ps[-2]$. Cc bn c th th nghim iu kin bin khng thm thu - wall: $\rho[0] = \rho[1], u[0] = -u[1], p[0] = p[1]$.

gii phng trnh f(P)=0 ta s dng th vin scipy (cc bn cn ci t trc khi s dng), hm fsolve hoc newton trong module optimize.

import numpy as np
import matplotlib.pyplot as plt
from scipy.optimize import fsolve, newton

#cc hng s: g - s m on nhit 
#m - minus, p - plus, d - divide 
g      = 1.4
gp1    = g+1
gm1    = g-1
gp1d2  = (g+1)/2
gm1d2  = (g-1)/2
gp1d2g = (g+1)/(2*g)
gm1d2g = (g-1)/(2*g)
gm1dgp1 = (g-1)/(g+1)
gdgm1  = g/(g-1)
g2dgm1 = g*2/(g-1)
eps    = 1e-6     #epsilon - sai s 

#phng php lp Godunov tm P
def pressure_classic_godunov(Pl, Pr):
    r1, u1, p1 = Pl[0], Pl[1], Pl[2]
    r2, u2, p2 = Pr[0], Pr[1], Pr[2]
    
    #vn tc m thanh 
    c1 = np.sqrt(g*p1/r1)
    c2 = np.sqrt(g*p2/r2)
    
    #phng php lp 
    P0 = (p1*r2*c2 + p2*r1*c1 + (u1-u2)*r1*c1*r2*c2)/(r1*c1+r2*c2)
    if P0 < eps: P0 = eps
    iterations = 50 # max_iteration 
    while (True):
        P = P0 #p sut P^{n-1}
        if P >= p1: a1 = np.sqrt(r1*(gp1d2*P + gm1d2*p1))
        else:
            pp = max(eps, P/p1)
            op = 1. - pp**gm1d2g
            if op>=eps: a1 = gm1d2g*r1*c1*(1. - pp)/op
            else: a1 = r1*c1
        if P >= p2: a2 = np.sqrt(r2*(gp1d2*P + gm1d2*p2))
        else:
            pp = max(eps, P/p2)
            op = 1. - pp**gm1d2g
            if op>=eps: a2 = gm1d2g*r2*c2*(1. - pp)/op
            else: a2 = r2*c2

        z = P/(p1+p2)
        alpha = gm1/(3*g)*(1. - z)/np.power(z, gp1d2g)/(1. - np.power(z, gm1d2g)) - 1.
        if alpha < 0.: alpha = 0.
        phi = (a2*p1 + a1*p2 + a1*a2*(u1 - u2))/(a1+a2)

        P0 = (alpha*P + phi)/(1. + alpha)#tnh P^n
        iterations -= 1
        if (abs(P0 - P) < eps) or (not iterations): break
    #kt thc vng lp! 
  
    return P, c1, c2, a1, a2

#gii phng trnh f(P) = 0 , tm P
def pressure_root_finding(Pl, Pr):
    r1, u1, p1 = Pl[0], Pl[1], Pl[2]
    r2, u2, p2 = Pr[0], Pr[1], Pr[2]
    
    #vn tc m thanh 
    c1 = np.sqrt(g*p1/r1)
    c2 = np.sqrt(g*p2/r2)

    fl_shock = lambda P: (P - p1)/(r1*c1*np.sqrt(gp1d2g*(P/p1) + gm1d2g))
    fl_expansion = lambda P: c1/gm1d2*((P/p1)**gm1d2g - 1.)
    fr_shock = lambda P: (P - p2)/(r2*c2*np.sqrt(gp1d2g*(P/p2) + gm1d2g))
    fr_expansion = lambda P: c2/gm1d2*((P/p2)**gm1d2g - 1.)
    
    def fl(P):
        if P >= p1: return fl_shock(P)
        else: return fl_expansion(P)
    def fr(P):
        if P >= p2: return fr_shock(P)
        else: return fr_expansion(P)
    
    f = lambda P: fl(P) + fr(P) + u2 - u1
    
    P,info, ier, msg = fsolve(f, (p1+p2)/2.,full_output=True,xtol=1.e-14)
    #trng hp sng gin mnh hay chn khng 
    if ier!=1:
        P,info, ier, msg = fsolve(f, (p1+p2)/2.,full_output=True,factor=0.1,xtol=1.e-10)
        # trng hp nghim khng hi t 
        if ier!=1: 
            print 'Warning: fsolve did not converge.'
            print msg
   
    if P >= p1-eps: a1 = np.sqrt(r1*(gp1d2*P + gm1d2*p1))
    else: a1 =  gm1d2g*r1*c1*(1. - P/p1)/(1. - np.power(P/p1, gm1d2g))
    if P >= p2-eps: a2 = np.sqrt(r2*(gp1d2*P + gm1d2*p2))
    else: a2 =  gm1d2g*r2*c2*(1. - P/p2)/(1. - np.power(P/p2, gm1d2g))
    
    return P, c1, c2, a1, a2

def riemann_exact_solution(Pl, Pr, pressure_solver, x, xstar, t_max):
    r1, u1, p1 = Pl[0], Pl[1], Pl[2]
    r2, u2, p2 = Pr[0], Pr[1], Pr[2]
    
    P, c1, c2, a1, a2 = pressure_solver(Pl, Pr)
    U = (a1*u1 + a2*u2 + p1 - p2)/(a1 + a2)
    #xt gin on bn tri 
    if P > p1: #nu l sng xung kch 
        D1 = u1 - a1/r1
        D1star = D1
        R1 = r1*a1/(a1-r1*(u1-U))
    else: #nu l sng gin
        D1 = u1 - c1
        c1star = c1 + gm1d2*(u1-U)
        D1star = U - c1star
        R1 = g*P/c1star**2
    #tng t cho gin on bn phi 
    if P > p2: #nu l sng xung kch 
        D2 = u2 + a2/r2
        D2star = D2
        R2 = r2*a2/(a2 + r2*(u2-U))
    else: #nu l sng gin
        D2 = u2 + c2
        c2star = c2 - gm1d2*(u2-U)
        D2star = U + c2star
        R2 = g*P/c2star**2
        
    #xt cu hnh phn r xc nh nghim PStar = (Rstar, Ustar, Pstar)
    #ty theo v tr bin i+1/2 nm trong vng no (xem bi 12)
    w = (x - xstar)/t_max
    P_out = np.zeros((3, len(x)))
    for i, wi in enumerate(w):
        if wi<=D1:   #nm bn tri sng tri
            P_out[0, i] = r1
            P_out[1, i] = u1
            P_out[2, i] = p1
        elif wi>=D2: #nm bn phi sng phi
            P_out[0, i] = r2
            P_out[1, i] = u2
            P_out[2, i] = p2
        elif D1star<= wi <= U: #nm gia hai sng 
            P_out[0, i] = R1
            P_out[1, i] = U
            P_out[2, i] = P
        elif U<= wi <= D2star: #nm gia hai sng 
            P_out[0, i] = R2
            P_out[1, i] = U
            P_out[2, i] = P
        elif D1< wi < D1star: #nm trong sng gin tri
            cstar = gm1dgp1*(u1 - wi) + c1/gp1d2
            P_out[1, i] = wi + cstar
            P_out[2, i] = p1*(cstar/c1)**g2dgm1           
            P_out[0, i] = g*P_out[2, i]/cstar**2
        elif D2star< wi < D2: #nm trong sng gin phi 
            cstar = gm1dgp1*(wi - u2) + c2/gp1d2
            P_out[1, i] = wi - cstar
            P_out[2, i] = p2*(cstar/c2)**g2dgm1          
            P_out[0, i] = g*P_out[2, i]/cstar**2
        else:
            print 'Decay Error!'
#         wave_speed = np.array([D1, D1star, U, D2, D2star])
    return P_out #, wave_speed

        D2star = U + c2star
        R2 = g*P/c2star**2
        
    #xt cu hnh phn r xc nh nghim PStar = (Rstar, Ustar, Pstar)
    #ty theo v tr bin i+1/2 nm trong vng no (xem bi 12)

    if D1>0 and D2>0:   #nm bn tri sng tri
        Rstar = r1
        Ustar = u1
        Pstar = p1
    elif D1<0 and D2<0: #nm bn phi sng phi
        Rstar = r2
        Ustar = u2
        Pstar = p2
    elif D1<0 and D2>0: #nm gia hai sng 
        if U>=0: Rstar = R1 #nm bn tri gin on tip xc
        else:    Rstar = R2 #nm bn phi gin on tip xc
        Ustar = U
        Pstar = P
    elif D1<0 and D1star>0: #nm trong sng gin tri
        Ustar = gm1dgp1*u1 + c1/gp1d2
        Pstar = p1*np.power(Ustar/c1, g2dgm1)
        Rstar = g*p1/Ustar**2   
    elif D2>0 and D2star<0: #nm trong sng gin phi 
        Ustar = gm1dgp1*u2 - c2/gp1d2
        Pstar = p2*np.power(Ustar/c2, g2dgm1)
        Rstar = g*p2/Ustar**2
    else:
        print 'Decay Godunov: Error!'
        
    #vector bin gc PStar
    PStar = [Rstar, Ustar, Pstar]
    return PStar

#hm chuyn i t P sang U 
def P2U(P, U):
    r, u, p = P[:, 0], P[:, 1], P[:, 2]
    U[:, 0] = r
    U[:, 1] = r*u
    U[:, 2] = r*u**2/2. + p/gm1
    return

#hm chuyn i t U v P
def U2P(U, P):
    r, ru, re = U[:, 0], U[:, 1], U[:, 2]
    P[:, 0] = r
    P[:, 1] = u = ru/r
    P[:, 2] = (re - ru*u/2.)*gm1
    return

#hm chuyn i t P sang F
def P2F(P, F):
    r, u, p = P[:, 0], P[:, 1], P[:, 2]
    F[:, 0] = r*u
    F[:, 1] = r*u**2 + p
    F[:, 2] = r*u**3/2. + p*u*gdgm1
    return

def time_step(P, CFL, dx):
    r, u, p = P[:, 0], P[:, 1], P[:, 2]
    c = np.sqrt(g*p/r) #vn tc m thanh 
    u_max = max(abs(u) + c)        
    dt = CFL*dx/u_max
    return dt

#ti cu trc nghim hng s tng mnh - Godunov
def godunov_reconstr(Ps):
    P_left = Ps[:-1].copy()
    P_right = Ps[1:].copy()
    return P_left, P_right

#s  godunov bc 1
def godunov_method(Ps, pressure_solver, nx, dx, CFL, time_target):
    Us = Ps.copy()
    P2U(Ps, Us)
    Pstar = Ps[:-1].copy()
    Fstar = Ps[:-1].copy()
    time = 0.0
    while(time < time_target):
        
        #bc 1: ti cu trc - reconstruction 
        P_left, P_right = godunov_reconstr(Ps)
        #bc 2: tm nghim phn r gin on, tnh hm dng
        for i in range(nx-1):
            Pstar[i] = decay_godunov(P_left[i], P_right[i], pressure_solver)
        P2F(Pstar, Fstar)
            
        #bc 3: tch phn theo thi gian
        dt = time_step(Ps, CFL, dx) 
        if(time+dt > time_target): dt = time + dt-time_target
        time += dt

        Us[1:-1] = Us[1:-1] - dt/dx*(Fstar[1:] - Fstar[:-1]) #tm bin bo ton 
        U2P(Us, Ps) #tm bin nguyn thy
        
        #iu kin bin: outflow
        Ps[0] = Ps[1]
        Ps[-1] = Ps[-2]        
    return

def riemann_solver(Pl, Pr, pressure_solver, x, xstar, t_max, CFL, test_name):
    nx = len(x)
    dx = x[1] - x[0] #xt trng hp li u 
    #to mng Ps - matrix (nx, 3)
    Ps = np.zeros((nx, 3))
    Ps[:int(xstar/dx)] = Pl
    Ps[int(xstar/dx):] = Pr

    godunov_method(Ps, pressure_solver, nx, dx, CFL, t_max)
    np.savetxt('data/euler_godunov_'+test_name+'.dat', Ps)
    
    #exact solution - matrix (3, nx)
    P_exact = riemann_exact_solution(Pl, Pr, pressure_solver, x, xstar, t_max)
    
    e_godunov   = Ps[:, 2]/(gm1*Ps[:, 0])
    e_exact     = P_exact[2]/(gm1*P_exact[0])
